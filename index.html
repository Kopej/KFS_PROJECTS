Welcome to our web application for predicting forest fires using remote sensing and GIS.

With a case study of Aberdare forest, we provide real-time data and analysis of periodic forest fires in the region. Our application leverages the latest remote sensing technologies to monitor the spread of forest fires and detect potential hotspots before they become out of control. We use advanced GIS spatial analysis techniques to provide accurate data on the intensity and direction of forest fires, enabling stakeholders to take necessary measures to prevent or minimize the damage caused by these fires. Our team of experts includes GIS and remote sensing professionals, IT specialists, and forest conservationists, who have come together to develop a comprehensive solution for predicting forest fires. The application has been thoroughly tested and approved by the Kenya Forest Service GIS Department, ensuring its accuracy and reliability. By using our web application, you can gain access to real-time data and analysis of forest fires in Aberdare forest and take necessary measures to protect the environment and the lives and livelihoods of people living in the area. Join us in our efforts to conserve the forests and protect our planet from the dangers of forest fires.
